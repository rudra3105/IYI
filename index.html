About The Event

MISSION

The mission of IYI’s conference is to inspire Indian youth to deliberate, debate, and discuss their ideas for India’s future, and in this undertaking begin the process of shaping the India of tomorrow.

VISION

The International Youth Initiative Conference intents at providing quality platform for students that bring together the next generation of international leaders. We prepare the participants to be better global citizens through excellent educational experiences that emphasize collaboration and cooperative resolution of conflict. We envision a world comprised of civically engaged people who strive for peaceful, multi-lateral conflict resolution and equitable and sustainable human development.
